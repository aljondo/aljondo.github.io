Americorps Boston

2016

For my Software Development course, I, along with a small group of other students, have been working closely with Americorps' Boston office to completely remake their website and volunteer matching app.

My team is in charge of designing and implementing the front-end. We have been part of every step of the process from drafting requirements, to creating mockups, and are currently developing the site using the React JavaScript library.

Check out the project's repo on Github!
