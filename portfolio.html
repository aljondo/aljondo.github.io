Professional Portfolio

Hey! My name is Ryan Aljondi. I am a web developer whose strengths lie in React and data visualization using D3. When a college course paired me with a non-profit called Little Brothers: Friends of the Elderly, I realized that I could use my skillset to help people in need and give back to my community. While I set myself up for a position to do even more good, my priority right now is to find a job with a company that is socially responsible and pays attention to important issues such as gender diversity in computer science.

I've seperated this portfolio into two sections. The first is a showcase of my professional and technical experience via the usual items: my resume and some personal and academic projects I've undertaken.

The second section contains some samples of writings from a college level Advanced Writing course I took. The papers demonstrate my stance on gender diversity in computer science by talking about the issue from different perspectives and with different audiences in mind.
