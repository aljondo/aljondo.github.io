Spotify Top 100 Visualizations

I used D3 to create a series of visualizations offering insight into my Spotify Top Tracks of 2016 playlist. You can view the same visualizations over your playlist as long as you're following your Top 100 playlist.

  1. Go to 2018 Wrapped and make sure to follow the playlist called "Your Top Songs 2018"

  2. Follow the playlist called "Your Top Songs 2018" and make sure it's public before hitting "Login to your Spotify" below. It might take a few minutes to work, so check out the visualizations of my playlist in the meantime.
