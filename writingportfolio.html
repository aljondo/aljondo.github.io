Writing samples

Proposal

This is a broad proposal regarding actions that can be taken to increase gender diversity in computer science, which, according to studies and statistics, has been decreasing for years across several metrics. This paper is a demonstration of recommending specific actions for a target audience to take, and backing up the argument with properly cited research.

Public Document

This is a press release from a fictional company called Writalics intended to reassure their target audience that they understand the importance of gender diversity, have taken actions to improve their own diversity, and are implementing further solutions for the future. This assignment demonstrates the ability to write about the issue from a corporate perspective, but in less formal and public facing manner.
